## Fourier Series

-   Analysis equation: $\begin{align}X(j\omega) = \int ^\infty_{-\infty} x(t) e^{-j\omega t}dt \end{align}$
-   Synthesis equation: $\begin{align} x(t) = \frac 1 {2\pi} \int ^{\infty}_{-\infty} X(j\omega) x^{j\omega t} d\omega \end{align}$


$E(\omega) = X(j\omega)$
