## Fourier Transform: Aperiodic Signals

$x[n] \to$ aperiodic DT signal.

Then we have the periodic extension: $\begin{align}x_N[n] = \sum^ \infty_{k=-\infty}x[n+kN]\end{align}$

So $x[n] = \lim_{N\to\infty} x_N[n]$
